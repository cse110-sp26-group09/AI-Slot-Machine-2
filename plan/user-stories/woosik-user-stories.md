# User Stories
**Researcher:** Woosik Kim  
**Date:** April 18, 2026

---

1. As a **casual player**, I want to **understand the paytable at a glance** so that **I know what symbols and combinations pay out without reading a manual**. *(clarity of game mechanics)*

2. As an **online gambler**, I want to **experience a realistic casino atmosphere from my phone** so that **I can get the thrill of casino gambling without having to travel to one**. *(satisfying feedback)*

3. As a **casual player**, I want to **control the spin button and adjust my bet easily** so that **I can play without feeling confused or overwhelmed by the interface**. *(ease of use)*

4. As a **casual player**, I want to **have my session progress saved automatically** so that **I don't lose my coins or bonus state if I accidentally close the tab**. *(accessibility of controls)*

5. As an **online gambler**, I want to **have a reason to come back daily** so that **the game stays engaging and I always have a new goal to work toward**. *(replay value and engagement)*
