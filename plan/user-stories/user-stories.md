**User Stories**

```

Researcher: Daniel Wu  
Date: April 19, 2026

1. As a casual player, I want to see a real-time summary of my session spend and net result so that I can make informed decisions about continuing to play without only depending on my balance display. (spend transparency)

2. As an adrenaline-driven player, I want a high-contrast, large-print interface mode with larger buttons and improved colour contrast so that I can play comfortably on my tablet without straining my eyes. (interface requirements)

3. As an online gambler, I look for the bonuses and want each game in the lobby to display its verified RTP percentage (mathematical transparency)

4. As a player whose a little hooked on gambling, I want responsible gambling prompts at meaningful decision points so that I am given opportunities to reflect on my play without feeling patronised. (responsible play)

5. As a casual player, I want to set a session budget before I play that automatically pauses the game when my net loss reaches my limit so that I never accidentally exceed my intended spend even during autoplay. (session control)

6. As a fervent player, I want to see my current loyalty tier, XP balance, and progress to the next tier clearly in my dashboard so that I feel recognised and motivated to engage with the platform's reward ecosystem. (loyalty visibility)

```

```

Researcher: Josh Victoria 
Date: April 19, 2026

1. As a player with not a lot of free time, I want to engage an "Auto-Play" or "Turbo" mode with pre-set loss limits, so that I can experience a high volume of spins and excitement without manual interaction for every round. (session limits and control)
   
2. As a player concerned about fairness, I want to access a simplified paytable and real-time "Win History" overlay, So that I can easily understand why a specific symbol combination resulted in a payout. (probability transparency)
   
3. As a player who just triggered a "Big Win," I want the app to initiate a multi-sensory celebration sequence (haptics, screen shakes, and coin fountains), So that the digital win feels as physically rewarding as a real-world payout. (sensory pleasent interface)
   
4. As a frequent player, I want my spins to contribute to a greater sense of progress eg.("New World Unlock","65% unlock until X reward), So that I feel a sense of accomplishment even during sessions where my coin balance decreases. (non-rapid stimuli based reward system)

5. As a player who wants to feel involved in the outcome, I want to be able to tap the screen to "Slam Stop" the reels, So that I feel like my timing and actions influence the win. ("control" over outcome)
```
