User Stories
Researcher: Daniel Wu  Date: April 19, 2026

1. As a casual player, I want to see a real-time summary of my session spend and net result so that I can make informed decisions about continuing to play without only depending on my balance display. *(spend transparency)*

2. As an adrenaline-driven player, I want a high-contrast, large-print interface mode with larger buttons and improved colour contrast so that I can play comfortably on my tablet without straining my eyes. *(interface requirements)*

3. As an online gambler, I look for the bonuses and want each game in the lobby to display its verified RTP percentage *(mathematical transparency)*

4. As a player whose a little hooked on gambling, I want responsible gambling prompts at meaningful decision points so that I am given opportunities to reflect on my play without feeling patronised. *(responsible play)*

5. As a casual player, I want to set a session budget before I play that automatically pauses the game when my net loss reaches my limit so that I never accidentally exceed my intended spend even during autoplay. *(session control)*

6. As a fervent player, I want to see my current loyalty tier, XP balance, and progress to the next tier clearly in my dashboard so that I feel recognised and motivated to engage with the platform's reward ecosystem. *(loyalty visibility)*
