## 2. Probabilities and Math

* **RTP (Return to Player):** The theoretical percentage of all wagered money that a slot pays back to players over time. 
   
* **Volatility (Variance):** * **Low Volatility:** Frequent, small wins. Keeps players engaged for longer periods .
* **Hit Frequency:** The percentage of spins that result in a win of any size. A hit frequency of 25% means a win roughly every 4 spins.
* **RNG (Random Number Generator):** A computer algorithm that ensures every spin is independent and mathematically random, making "hot" or "cold" streaks purely coincidental.

## 3. Psychological Hooks and Addiction
Slot machines are often cited as the "most addictive" form of gambling due to specific behavioral triggers.

* **Variable Ratio Reinforcement:** This is the most powerful trigger of addiction. Because rewards are delivered at unpredictable intervals, the brain remains in a constant state of alert and anticipation. This creates a hyper-fixation on the "next spin," as the player believes a win could happen at any moment.
* **The Near-Miss Effect:** Slot machines are engineered to trigger the brain's reward system. Every win—or even a "near-miss" releases a surge of dopamine. This chemical rush reinforces the behavior, turning the game into a physical craving.
* **The Illusion of Control:** Mechanics like the "Slam Stop" or "Bonus Picks" trick the player into believing their skill or timing influences the outcome. However, the result is entirely predetermined by a computer (RNG), but this illusion keeps the player emotionally invested and convinced they can "beat" the machine.

## 4. Common Terminology
| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Paytable** | The chart showing the value of each symbol combination. |
| **Payline** | A payline is the line across the reels where matching symbols must land to trigger a win. |
| **Wild** | A "Joker" symbol that substitutes for others to create a win. |
| **Scatter** | A symbol that triggers a bonus or payout regardless of where it lands on the reels. |
| **Cascading Reels** | Winning symbols disappear and new ones fall in, allowing for multiple wins comes with a maximum payout|
| **Megaway Reels** | More winning symbols added to the reels for more potential payouts|
| **Expanding Reels** | reel grows in size when special feature activates, for more symbols for potenial bigger payouts|
| **Multipliers** | Features that multiply the standard payout by a specific amount (2x, 10x, etc.). |
| **Variable Paylines** | Ability to change paylines, but have added downside of higher minimum bet|
| **Bet Level** | The amount of currency wagered per spin. |
| **Bonus Round** | A separate mini-game triggered by scatters, often with higher win potential. |
