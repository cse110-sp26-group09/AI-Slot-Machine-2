## Persona 3: Linda Vance, The Social Butterfly

| **Age** | 58 |
|---|---|
| **Occupation** | Semi-retired Office Administrator |
| **Device** | iPad (primary), Desktop PC |
| **Play Style** | Daily 1 to 2 hour sessions, mostly evenings while watching TV |
| **Experience** | Veteran of social casinos and bingo halls, transitioning to real-money community slots |

### Background
Linda grew up enjoying the lively atmosphere of weekend bingo halls and local casino trips with her friends. As those outings became less frequent, she turned to online platforms to fill the gap. She views her gambling budget strictly as an entertainment expense, similar to paying for a movie ticket or joining a bowling league. For Linda, the actual slot mechanics are secondary to the community. She plays primarily to chat, share virtual gifts, and participate in team leaderboards.

### What She Values
* **Community Integration:** In-game chat rooms, team tournaments, and the ability to send or receive free spins with friends.
* **Public Recognition:** Broadcasted wins where other players in the lobby can congratulate her.
* **Low Volatility:** Steady, small wins that keep her bankroll alive so she can stay in the chat room longer without having to reload.
* **Nostalgic Themes:** Classic fruit machines, bingo crossovers, and familiar television game show properties.

### What Frustrates Her
* **Isolation:** Games that take up the full screen and hide the community chat or friend list.
* **Toxicity:** Overly competitive, aggressive, or rude players in the social feeds.
* **High Minimum Bets:** Being forced to bet large amounts just to qualify for community jackpots or team points.

### How She Interprets Feedback
* Celebrates other players' big wins when they pop up in the feed, treating it as a shared community victory.
* Views losing streaks as just part of the cost of hanging out, often joking about the "cold machine" with her online friends.
* Highly motivated by cooperative progress bars, such as a "Community Jackpot" that the whole room is contributing toward together.
