| **Play Style** | 3–4 sessions per week, evenings and weekends, 30–45 min sessions |

### Background
Marcus works long hours at an accounting firm and often do online slots as a way to decompress after a stressful day. He discovered casino apps a few years ago and now plays regularly, mostly Marvel and Egyptian-themed slots. He often sets a mental budget of $60–80 a month, though he occasionally loses track of time during autoplay sessions and feels guilty afterward.

### What He Values
- Mobile experience he can pick up and put down easily
- Branded and visually stunning themes that make sessions feel like entertainment
- Loyalty rewards and tier progression that make him feel recognised
- Occasional jackpot potential, the anticipation of a big multiplier even though he knows is improbable
- Transparency about where his money is going (balance, bet amount, net profit/loss)

### What Frustrates Him
- Bonus rounds that feel out of reach on his budget
- Unintuitive UI where it feels difficult to cash out
- Losing track of spend during autoplay with no easy way to review it

### How He Interprets Feedback
- Win animations feel rewarding and reinforce his session enjoyment
- Near-misses read as motivation
- Timer popping up  mid-bonus, but he'd appreciate a spend summary he can check on his own terms
- A loyalty XP gain notification after a session makes him feel the time was worthwhile


## Persona 2: Jason Hermandre — The Bonus Hunter

| **Age** | 24 |
|---|---|
| **Occupation** | Computer Science Graduate Student |
| **Device** | Gaming PC (primary), Android phone |
| **Play Style** | 2–3 sessions per week, late nights, 45–60 min sessions |
| **Experience** | Analytically driven; researches RTP and bonus terms before registering anywhere |

### Background
Jaiden approaches online slots as a business where his RTP can be optimized. He found online casinos through a Discord community focused on gains and quickly developed his playstyle focused on tracking wager progress, eligible games, and withdrawal windows. He rotates platforms every 4–6 weeks after clearing a welcome bonus, and has a nuanced relationship with gambling: he understands the house inclined RTP but still plays regularly.

### What He Values
- High-RTP game libraries for the slot engine with transparent and verified RNG.
- Access to certified RNG audit documentation, provably fair mechanics matter to him
- Efficient gameplay mechanics like buy-feature and turbo-spin that respect his time
- A community of like-minded players to discuss strategy and platform comparisons

### What Frustrates Him
- Mathematically exploitative wagering requirements
- High-RTP titles excluded from bonus play without prominent disclosure upfront
- Bad cashout withdrawal mechanisms that feel designed to discourage cashing out
- Opacity around RNG implementation, he wants verifiable fairness

### How He Interprets Feedback
- Win animations are noise to him — he watches the balance number, not the celebration
- A progress bar showing 74% of wagering requirement complete is motivating since he treats it like a task to finish
- A platform that shows him the certified RTP on the game
